BSL Sentence Structure

BSL has a different sentence structure to both English. As the most basic linguistic analysis, BSL is described as having a topic-comment sign order. Throw the ball being signed – Ball, throw. Using commonly applied linguistic terminology to identify sentence structure, English and BSL have the following sentence structure: • English: Subject – Verb – Object (The boy rides a bike) • BSL: Object – Subject – Verb (bike the boy rides)

Please keep this important design in mind if you are trying to practise phrases or learning BSL as this will help maximise your communication experience with others.
